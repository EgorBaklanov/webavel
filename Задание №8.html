WATERPROOF
BATHROOM TVs
integrated vibration speakers
waterproof tv
All AVEL Bathroom TVs are certified with dust- and water protection IP rate standard. Test is performed in an authorized laboratory; test reports and certificates are issued for the series. Check IP rating in the product specification.
waterproof remote control
Currently we equip all our Bathroom TVs with IP66 waterproof remote controls, it means they can be rinsed and washed under water tap but can not be immersed in water.
vibration speakers
One of our unique features is resonance/vibration integrated speaker. This type of speakers works through the front glass of our TVs and there are no holes at all. That’s really a great benefit for design and waterproof concerns.
smart tv (option)
All our Bathroom TVs are compatible with smart TV boxes (Amazon TV, Xiaomi TV Box, Apple TV etc.) and support 2-way communication by HDMI CEC for the single remote control operation. AVEL TVs can be optionally equipped with Screen Mirroring function to share video and pictures from a smartphone by WiFi (AirPlay, Miracast, DLNA).
easy to wash
Front finish of our TVs is a plain real glass (safety glass for most of the models). It very easy to clean, even in kitchen or bathroom environment when there are water or steam on the screen.
full hd ips led
We choose the best quality LED LCD panels with wide viewing angles (IPS or VA technology). 100% incoming material test is performed before we start the assembling process. Full HD is our standard for small and average models, 4K is coming soon for 43” size and up.
digital tv system
Enjoy free HD digital quality broadcast channels or use CI / CI+ slot for pay TV channels! Our main TV board used for DVB standard supports DVB-T/T2/C/S2, Freeview and the latest H.264/H.265 codecs. DVB (Digital Video Broadcast) standard is used in Europe, Russia, Middle East, Australia, Africa and many countries worldwide.
2 year warranty
High quality Bathroom TVs from AVEL have 2-year unlimited warranty. Thorough multi-step testing and quality check procedures during and after assembling process let us make 100% sure the products will be sent and delivered to our customers in a perfect technical condition and will work for years.
